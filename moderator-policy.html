MODERATOR POLICY

As updated on DD MONTH YYYY
a)
Users can create topics on the Platform for other Users to post Content related to the topic created (which can include texts, audio content and/or images). Such creator of topic shall by default be the “Moderator” for the topic created ( “Moderated Topic” ). Moderators are important to the Storiyoh OneMic Ecosystem and in order to have some consistency in the creation of Moderated Topics and User Content uploaded under specific Moderated Topics and to ensure an open, friendly, harassment-free and welcoming atmosphere we expect acceptable behaviours from Moderators and Users.
b)
To enable maximum participation, we advise that Moderated Topics created are clear and concise. Moderator may also provide tips, links or explanations related to the Moderated Topic to assist Users to find what they are looking for on OneMic and post User Content related to the Moderated Topic. Users shall also have the option to follow a Moderator or a particular topic without commenting or uploading any Content.
c)
The Moderator who has created a Moderated Topic, can invite other Users to be the Moderator of his/her topic and/or form a Moderator group for the topic community, who are around to manage their topic and engage with the Storiyoh admin.
d)
Moderator’s email address will be visible to other Users and Storiyoh may contact a Moderator at the said email address or at the phone number registered with Storiyoh, should it so require to do so for any reason.
e)
Moderator shall ensure that the Moderated Topic and the Content uploaded by Users under the Moderated Topic conform to our Community Guidelines and the Storiyoh Terms in general and shall maintain a basic etiquette while posting Moderated Topic and interacting with Users who have posted User Content under the said topic . As the Moderator, always remember (i) to always be calm and polite even when Users are not; (ii) to not be rude to Users; and (iii) to not abuse your power based on your own opinion .
f)
Moderator shall have the right at his/her discretion to delete any User Content posted under the Moderated Topic created by him/her if the Moderator believes that the same violates the Storiyoh Terms (including but not limited to the Community Guidelines) or is objectionable in any way.
g)
Any User can report/flag any User Content which he/she finds offensive or believes to be a violation of the law or the Storiyoh Terms (including the Community Guidelines). Apart from flagging the objectionable post, a User may also report his/her concern to Storiyoh in the “Help & Support” link on the website.
h)
If any User Content under the Moderated Topic is flagged by another User or if it is found to be against Storiyoh Terms, Storiyoh shall have the option to intimate the Moderator for taking necessary action against the flagged User Content. A User whose User Content is flagged, the Moderator or Storiyoh can delete/remove the flagged User Content. Moderator shall endeavor to intimate the User if his/her User Content is being removed/deleted.
i)
Notwithstanding anything contained herein, Storiyoh, may delete/remove any User Content, including the Moderated Topic, in its absolute discretion.
j)
Moderated Topics may be rated by Storiyoh OneMic based on number of posts put up under the Moderated Topics, number of comments and ‘upvotes’ received and such other statistical or other criteria as deemed appropriate by Storiyoh and Moderated Topics may appear on the Storiyoh OneMic Platform based on the order of their rating.
k)
Storiyoh OneMic may suggest topics for Users, which shall only be considered as suggestions from OneMic and not as an endorsement of any kind.
l)
For more details on what User Content (including Moderated Topics) and general etiquette on the Platform is acceptable or is objectionable and/or is a violation of Storiyoh Terms, kindly refer to our Community Guidelines
Should you wish to report or make a complaint about Content which You believe Storiyoh should remove or take down from the Platform, kindly send us an email stating your reasons for the take-down request to legal@storiyoh.com .
